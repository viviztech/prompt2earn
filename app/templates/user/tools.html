💡 Tips for Audio / Voiceover Submissions

  • • Export as MP3 (preferred) or WAV. Keep file under 50MB
  • • Read the prompt — it will specify language (Tamil, Hindi, English) and tone (formal, friendly, energetic)
  • • Use ElevenLabs or Murf for the most natural-sounding voices
  • • For Tamil/regional language prompts, use TTSMaker or Narakeet — they handle Indian scripts well
  • • Keep the voiceover between 30 seconds and 3 minutes unless the prompt specifies otherwise
  • • Listen to your audio before uploading — check for unnatural pauses or mispronunciations
